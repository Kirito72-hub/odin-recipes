omelet

Description

Nothing beats the comfort that a classic ham and cheese omelette gives. The ham and cheese oozing inside of the omelette is just pure satisfaction. The added herbs in the eggs take it to the next level. This is more than just comfort food. It’s a giant hug, a shoulder to cry on, and your most avid cheerleader. That’s how much it can uplift your spirits.

Ingredients

  • Beat eggs
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. HEAT butter in 7 to 10-inch nonstick omelet pan or skillet over medium-high heat until hot
  2. GENTLY PUSH cooked portions from edges toward the center with inverted turner so that uncooked eggs can reach the hot pan surface
  3. When top surface of eggs is thickened and no visible liquid egg remains, PLACE filling on one side of the omelet. FOLD omelet in half with turner. With a quick flip of the wrist, turn pan and INVERT or SLIDE omelet onto plate
